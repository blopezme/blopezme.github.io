Practical Upgrades: Where to Spend Your Money

Choosing what to upgrade depends on your use case. Here's a short guide:

Everyday performance (web, office apps)

  • Add RAM to handle more tabs/apps
  • Switch to an SSD for faster boot and app load times

Gaming & Creative Work

  • Upgrade GPU for higher frame rates and faster rendering
  • Consider a faster CPU for CPU-bound games and video encoding

Other tips

  • Always check motherboard compatibility before buying parts
  • Buy a good-quality power supply to protect your components
